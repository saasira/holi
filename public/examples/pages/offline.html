Offline Indicator

Verify offline state, retries, and queue count

This page polls a dedicated uncached heartbeat endpoint every 2.5 seconds, so disconnecting your network should surface the indicator without relying on cached page responses.

    Public API

    • <offline>, [component="offline"], and [role="offline"] are supported declaration forms.
    • scope: page, block, or inline.
    • position: top-left, top-right, bottom-left, bottom-right, top-center, bottom-center.
    • ping-url, heartbeat-ms, probe-timeout-ms, duration, and max-attempts configure polling and visibility.
    • JS helpers: el.offlineIndicator.retryConnection(), simulateOffline(), simulateOnline(), OfflineIndicator.queue(payload), OfflineIndicator.clearQueue(), and OfflineIndicator.create(options).
